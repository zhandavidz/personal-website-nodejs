Newton's Third Law of Motion

Second Law

Newton's Third Law of Motion States that:

For every action, there is an equal and opposite reaction.

Now, we have a very short law, but also the hardest law to understand.

Explanation:

Connection to Swimming:

What this law means is that for each force, an exactly opposite one exists.
